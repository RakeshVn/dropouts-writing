8 Purposes why you should be writing a book if you have thought of it

  • Admin
  • Jun 01, 2020

Have you always fascinated how your ideas, thoughts or stories could make for a great book?

If yes, mark the beginning of something meaningful, in an otherwise meaningless existence. Here are all the purposes why you should author a book right away, even if you do not have the time or literary skills needed!

You become immortal

Write about what has lived to those who will come to live, and just like that, you are eternized. That is just our crafty way of saying you are what you have made yourself, and if that ‘self’ has an idea, a thought, a story to tell to the world, you will live on forever, lauded and rooted for. Do you not feel we are just memories in someone’s head? Why not make it a pleasant one? Write, and your ideas will live forever in words, and you, forever in readers’ minds.

We may not have reached the digital upload of your mind, but the essence of who you are will remain in scriptures.

World needs stories

A story isn't just a book, a movie or lores of 'Very important people'. It's everywhere, and every story has something to say to the world. What are you doing when you are not working, watching something or scrolling through social media? What are you doing when you talk to people? You are talking stories. Stories are all we live for. And they must be told. It could be that of success, that of inspiration or that of love and relationships. No matter what the story arc is, it's all rooted in the stem of humanity.

You foot-smash a wave into the cosmos

A wave of something that happened, something that was real. You. Pointlessly do people believe they are just insignificant. You are insignificant, maybe a fact. You becoming significant is a possibility, a beautiful one. It could be a ripple or a Tsunami, but with your existence, you send a wave out for sure. Now, for someone to understand a wave, they must study it, right? Write a book, and give them material!

Let the world know what meant to you the most

It could be your learnings, it could be your self-help ideas, it could be your success story; they mean to you the most. And there's no harm in sharing with people what means to you the most. It is always good to share, isn't it?

Tell those things that you otherwise couldn't

That's the beauty of thoughts. It gives you wings to fly away from the mundane worldly confines beneath. And trust me, the view is great from up here! With a book, you could express all your unabridged thoughts and understandings of the world. Wanna bleed out your unspoken pain? You can do that too!

Make a difference

You will make a difference. You might inspire one, and him, hundreds. Your ideas are like a ray of light that manifests out of you and travels into the universe, and it will shine upon things. You may not live to see it, but know that the others would be able to see things better.

Share your experiences

To those people that matter to you, to those that are out there. Tell them about your vulnerabilities, glories, excitement, happiness. Tell them things you've experienced, tell them how human you felt. It's cathartic to you and endearing to them. Your moments are now in a mason jar, preserved for the future to savour on.

Author a book, even if you aren’t a writer

What you have got to say is all that matters, and that is the purpose for anyone to write. It is okay if you are not a writer, or you do not have time to write.
